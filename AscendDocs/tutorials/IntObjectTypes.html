    IntObject Types

    IntSolid

    • Anything that acts as ground that an actor or the player can collide with.
    • This object type is intended for static solids. For moveable solids use IntMoveableSolid, which is a subclass of IntSolid.
    • Collisions:
      • Player: Block
      • Actors: Block
      • Auras: No
      • Solids: No
      • Triggers: No

    IntActor

    • Any "entity" that moves around the world with physics and collides with the world.
    • Actors can optionally collide with the Player, other actors or triggers. This can be configured by passing in callbacks to SimpleMove, or any of the other movement functions. To ignore collisions, simply pass null. Note that OnCollideX and OnCollideY are required and cannot be null.
    SimpleMove(velocity, OnCollideX, OnCollideY, OnActor, OnTrigger);
    • By default, all actors hurt the Player upon collision. To prevent this, add the "Docile" tag to the owning GameObject
    • Collisions:
      • Player: Overlap (optional)
      • Actors: Overlap (optional)
      • Auras: No
      • Solids: Block
      • Triggers: Overlap (optional)

    IntTrigger

    • Anything in the scene that does not require physical collisions. Mostly used to detect overlap with pre-defined regions.
    • Used for player's weapons and interactables.
    • Technically Auras are also triggers, but they have a lot of custom functionality going on under the hood.
